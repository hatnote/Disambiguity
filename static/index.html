Gazelle

Closely related to the true gazelles are the Tibetan and Mongolian gazelles (species of the genus Procapra), the blackbuck of Asia, and the African impala and Springbok. One widely familiar gazelle is the African species Thomson's Gazelle (Eudorcas thomsoni), which is around 52 to 67 cm (20 to 26 in) in height at the shoulder and is coloured brown and white with a distinguishing black stripe (as in the picture on the right).
